Recipe


Some people think it's weird to eat frogs but they are actually really good. Usually you only eat the legs and for most poeple it tastes like chicken.
So I hope you try them with this recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Frog Legs
  • Flour/Fish Fry Batter
  • Eggs
  • Oil(For Frying)
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Salt & Pepper

Cooking:

  1. Thaw frog legs, if frozen; seperate into individual legs. Then dip in beaten egg.
  2. In a bowl mix flour, salt, pepper, and cajun or premade dry batter.
  3. Coat legs in dry mix.
  4. In a pan or deep fryer, heat 3 inches of shortening or cooking oil to 375 degrees. Fry a few legs at a time for ~2 min or until done.
  5. Dry on paper towels and enjoy!
